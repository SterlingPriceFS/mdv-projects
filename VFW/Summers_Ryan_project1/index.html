The Epic Tale of Your Life and the Quests You Undertake

You may not have known it, but the everyday life you lead is not the boring existence it may seem at first glance. The daily tasks you complete are actually Quests. Completing your Quests actually requires you use your Skills, and they progress the more you use them. You aren't just walking through each day of a mundane existence; you are actually at the beginning point of your epic journey. Enter your daily Quests with as much or as little detail as you like.
